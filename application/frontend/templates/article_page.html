Tab description (click to expand)

Explore the original Wikipedia article you have chosen in the frame below. You can browse to other articles and return to the chosen article by pressing Return to article. Note that by right clicking on any wikipedia link while exploration you can copy the link name and use its last part, which is the unique name of the article, in order to select another article. To do this, you can press Change article in the top of the page and then paste the copied link into the search field and deleting the link details except for the mentioned article name.

After the preloading process is finished you can select any revision of the article by entering the Revision date. The input field has auto-complete function helping to select one of the existing dates. Start inputing the year, for example 2012 and continue selecting the date in the format yyyymmddhhmmss
